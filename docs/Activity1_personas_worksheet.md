# Activity 1: User Personas - GDM Chatbot
## CS 584 Case Study

**Instructions:** Create 2 distinct patient personas representing different types of GDM patients in Kerala.

---

## PERSONA 1

**Name:** Priya-like people

### DEMOGRAPHICS
- **Age:** 20s-30s
- **Location:** urban
- **Occupation:** employed (ex. tech-related)
- **Pregnancy:** 20 weeks, 1st pregnancy (1st/2nd/3rd)
- **Lives with:** husband
- **Languages:** Malayalam, English

### TECHNOLOGY
- **Device:** phone
- **Digital Literacy:** High 
- **Apps Used:** social media
- **Can type easily:** Yes 
- **Prefers:** Text / In-person

### GOALS (What do they want to accomplish?)
1. Understand diet rules related to her culture 
2. Automatic logging
3. Help family understand 

### PAIN POINTS (What makes GDM management difficult?)
1. Family does not understand
2. Manual logging is time-consuming
3. Doctor is busy

### KEY QUOTE FROM INTERVIEW
> "Why can't we just use phone, logbook is too tedious."
> "Too much time to travel to clinic for basic advice."
> "Culturally irrelevant advice for diet. "

### TOP 3 NEEDS FROM GDM CHATBOT
1. automatic tracker
2. culturally relevant diet advice
3. replace basic advice from doctor

## PERSONA 2

**Name:** Lakshmi-like people

### DEMOGRAPHICS
- **Age:** 30s
- **Location:** rural
- **Occupation:** homemaker
- **Pregnancy:** 20 weeks, 3rd pregnancy (1st/2nd/3rd)
- **Lives with:** extended family
- **Languages:** Malayalam

### TECHNOLOGY
- **Device:** traditional phone
- **Digital Literacy:**  Low
- **Apps Used:** None (just calls)
- **Can type easily:** No
- **Prefers:** Voice / In-person

### GOALS (What do they want to accomplish?)
1. Understand diabetes in her own language
2. How to use blood sugar machine
3. Increase family literacy on diabetes

### PAIN POINTS (What makes GDM management difficult?)
1. Cannot operate a smartphone
2. No reliable internet 
3. Travel and language barriers

### KEY QUOTE FROM INTERVIEW
> "Hospital is far and no one explained how to use glucose monitor"
> "Can't read English"
> "Other family members cook and are not familiar with diabetes-related diet" 

### TOP 3 NEEDS FROM GDM CHATBOT
1. Voice-assistant to explain blood sugar machine
2. Voice + native language support for diet advice
3. Needs to work offline
