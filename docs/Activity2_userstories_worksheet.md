# Activity 2: User Stories - GDM Chatbot
## CS 584 Case Study

**Instructions:** Write 3 user stories for each persona you created.

**Format:** As [persona name], I want [goal], So that [benefit].

---

## EXAMPLES

**Example 1:**
> As Urban Priya,
> I want to log my glucose with one tap and a photo of my meal,
> So that I can quickly track during my work lunch break.

**Example 2:**
> As Rural Lakshmi,
> I want to record my glucose using voice in Malayalam,
> So that I can track without typing, which is difficult for me.

---

## PERSONA 1: Priya-like

### Story 1:
**As** Priya,

**I want** to use my smartphone to easily log my blood sugar,

**So that** my husband is also aware.


### Story 2:
**As** Priya,

**I want** to communicate with an AI chatbot,

**So that** I can receive basic and reliable health information.


### Story 3:
**As** Priya,

**I want** a general education app,

**So that** my family is aware.

---

## PERSONA 2: Lakshmi

### Story 1:
**As** Lakshmi,

**I want** voice-assistant support,

**So that** I can understand how to properly use the blood sugar machine.


### Story 2:
**As** Lakshmi,

**I want** a chatbot in my native language,

**So that** I can learn about diet for diabetes.


### Story 3:
**As** Lakshmi,

**I want** a system that works offline,

**So that** I don't need to travel so far and can receive health advice at home.
